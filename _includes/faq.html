Will my server be 24/7?

Each server has an uptime limit of 5 hours irrespective of the number of players online, therefore each server will be shutdown 5 hours after it has been started, servers will also shutdown when no player joins within 20 minutes after the server has been started, or when the last player leaves and no player joins within 10 minutes.

Why is my country blacklisted?

If we don't support your country as of now, then it fails in terms of economy therefore Ads in your country don't provide us good amount of income which is required to maintain the nodes.

Is FiveM Supported?

FalixNodes Limited is a profit organization company, hence FiveM does not allow us to host FiveM servers. Due to FiveM's policy, we are not permitted to host their servers.
